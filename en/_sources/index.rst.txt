Grace Hopper Computing Cluster
==============================

.. important::

   - Researchers in a hurry should at least read this page and the :doc:`summary
     <summary>` before running their first job.
   - Cette documentation est également disponible en `français
     <https://ccs-udes.github.io/hpc-gh/fr/>`_.

The Grace Hopper (GH) Computing Cluster is a high-performance computing (HPC)
service for `Université de Sherbrooke <https://www.usherbrooke.ca/>`_ (UdeS)
researchers who contribute one or more GH servers. The cluster is also usable by
collaborators of contributing researchers. The GH Cluster is managed by the
`Centre de calcul scientifique <https://www.ccs.usherbrooke.ca/>`_ (CCS) at the
UdeS.

The GH Cluster is configured differently from the national clusters and other
UdeS clusters, particularly with regards to the Linux environment and scientific
software.

The GH Cluster is a specialised resource for GPU-accelerated computations, such
as machine learning and other fields of study of artificial intelligence.

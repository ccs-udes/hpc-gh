Account
=======

Institut quantique researchers
------------------------------

If you are a researcher at the `Institut quantique
<https://www.usherbrooke.ca/iq/en/>`_ (IQ), get an account on the `IQ Cluster
<https://ccs-udes.github.io/hpc-iq/en/>`_. Once your account is activated, you
will automatically also gain access to the GH Cluster if your research group
contributes one or more GH servers.

Bioinformatics service staff
----------------------------

If you work for the `Bioinformatics service
<https://bioinfo.ccs.usherbrooke.ca/>`_ of the `Biology Department
<https://www.usherbrooke.ca/biologie/>`_, ask your supervisor about access to
the GH cluster.
